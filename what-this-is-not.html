What Reytech Reserve Is / Is Not

Reytech Reserve is built for order, discipline, and capital preservation — not for speculation. Below are the boundaries in plain language.

What this IS saving-first

  • A commission-based digital savings framework. No interest model.
  • A rules-based structure where terms are predefined and disclosed.
  • User funds are segregated. Operational funds are not mixed with user funds.
  • Designed for long-term behavior: reduce impulse decisions and emotional exits.
  • Fees are clear. 0% entry, 0.1% monthly service fee + 1% exit commission.

What this is NOT no confusion

  • Not a bank. Not a deposit product.
  • Not an investment product. No ROI promises, no profit expectations.
  • Not trading. No leverage, no margin, no “signals”, no speculation.
  • Not lending. No loans, no interest-bearing instruments.
  • Not a token sale. No token/coin promise, no “price” narrative.
